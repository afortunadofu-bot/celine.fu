02 — How I Think02 — 思維方式

EVERY PROBLEM
IS A STRUCTURE.每個問題都是
一個結構。

Approach · Framework · Mindset方法 · 框架 · 心態
01
DEFINE BEFORE
YOU DIVE先定義
再深入

The most expensive analytics mistake: answering the wrong question precisely. I frame the decision before touching data.分析工作最昂貴的錯誤是精準地回答了錯誤的問題。我先框定決策，再碰數據。

02
BREAK INTO
TESTABLE LAYERS拆解成
可測試層次

"Why did revenue drop?" → volume vs. price vs. mix vs. timing. Mutually exclusive layers find real drivers 3× faster.「為什麼收入下降？」→ 量 vs. 價 vs. 結構 vs. 時序。互斥層次找到真正驅動因素快 3 倍。

03
INSIGHT MUST
DRIVE ACTION洞察必須
接到行動

Analysis that doesn't change behavior is entertainment. Every deliverable ends: "Here's what this means. Here's what I recommend next."不改變行為的分析只是娛樂。每份交付物都以「這代表什麼，以及建議下一步」作為結尾。
